REGION 1: GATEWAY TO THE NORTH

Welcome to Region 1, the Ilocos Region — where history, culture, and natural beauty meet! From the stone streets of Vigan in Ilocos Sur to the golden beaches of La Union, the lush landscapes of Pangasinan, and the scenic views of Ilocos Norte, Region 1 promises unforgettable adventures at every turn. Whether you crave thrilling escapades, peaceful retreats, or a deep dive into the Philippines’ colorful past, the Ilocos Region is ready to welcome you with open arms and endless stories to discover.
